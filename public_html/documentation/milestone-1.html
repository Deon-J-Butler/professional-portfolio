PWP Milestone 1

Purpose:

This project will be a professional portfolio website that will focus on showcasing development skills to potential employers. It will feature information about myself, my career, and my goals. There will also be links to GitHub and LinkedIn for networking.

Audience:

This site is designed for professional patrons within both the Intelligence and IT communities. It will be included in GitHub and LinkedIn profiles for recruiters, hiring agents, professional network, and employers to view.

Goal:

The goal of this website is to serve as a tool to complement my resume by sending to employers to showcase my development work. It will also be a networking tool by linking to professional networking websites and including a contact form for interested parties to contact me directly.

Persona:

Chance N. Deas

Profession:

42y.o hiring manager that has been working at Verizon for the past 12 years. Reviewing applications and online professional portfolios of fullstack web and software developers. Looking to hire developers to improve UI/UX with Verizon.

Technology:

Cellphone: iPhone 13 Pro - A15 Bionic chip, 4-core GPU, 5G cellular, iOS 15.4.1, 256GB

  • Browser(s): Safari

Home Computer: MacBook Air - Apple M1 chip with 8-core CPU, 7-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine, macOS 12.0 Monterey, 256GB SSD

  • Browser(s): Safari, Mozilla Firefox

Work Computer: iMac - Apple M1 chip with 8-core CPU, 7-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine, macOS 12.0 Monterey, 512GB SSD

  • Browser(s): Safari, Mozilla Firefox

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Patient hiring manager that has onboarded numerous talented individuals in the past. Is known to send emails with follow-up questions and conduct telephonic/video interviews after receiving applications. Wants to get to know the person behind the application. Due to years of experience with the company, is very conscious of what type of person can fill the role the company needs and can efficiently build an ideal employee profile and seek the individual.

Frustrations and Needs:

Chance needs to hire someone with a strong, foundational understanding of web development. The ideal recruit will have at least a Bachelor's in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field and at least 2 years of experience (a degree can be waived for applicants with 4+ years of experience). Chance is also looking for someone with strong soft skills being able to communicate effectively with external departments, advise senior leaders as a SME, support design teams, and deliver user satisfaction. Applicants must also be a quick learner and problem solver, and is expected to be able to take initiative to accomplish tasks with minimal to no guidance. Wants to hire a new employee by the end of the month.

Goals:

Chance is extremely efficient in identifying whether an applicant possesses the necessary skills for the role. Can Chance get a good idea of who this person is by viewing their website? Does the website showcase enough high-level skill that demonstrates the persons capabilities with development? Does this website provide access to other developments this person has created that can further showcase their skills? Does this website contain a way for Chance to contact the applicant with follow-up questions? Chance is determined to find the right talent and wants to thoroughly get to know what the applicant's qualifications and capabilities are.

User Story:

As a hiring manager, I need to be able to get an accurate idea of applicants' qualifications so that I can hire the best fit for the role.
